The following is a selection of my tools that I use for my professional work. Full disclosure, the links to the resources can be affiliate links or referral links.

### Editor

- [Visual Studio Code](https://github.com/laurosilvacom/dotfiles)
- [Personal dotfiles.](https://github.com/laurosilvacom/dotfiles) For macOS: zsh, iterm, and more.
- [Night Owl](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=sdras.night-owl) Main editor theme.

### Office

- [The LG UltraFine 5K Display.](https://amzn.to/2AcPCku) Great for recording screencasts.
- [Bose QuietComfort 35.](https://amzn.to/3h06trq) Great for the home office and travel.
- [MacBook Pro (15-inch, 2019). ](https://amzn.to/2YcC9S1)Main laptop.
- [Versare](https://www.versare.com/shop/portable-partitions/acoustical-room-dividers/versifold-acoustical-room-divider.html) - a lightweight folding acoustic room divider.

- [Shure BETA 87A](https://amzn.to/3eRA2JQ) - the official mic for egghead instructors.

- [K&M 23850 Microphone Desk Arm](https://amzn.to/2YcI3SU) - high quality microphone boom arm.
